## Overview

GPT-Prompter transforms the way you interact with AI tools by giving you structured, centralized control over your prompts. Instead of rewriting prompts from scratch or losing your best inputs in chat history, GPT-Prompter turns your prompts into a **scalable, reusable professional asset**.

Whether you're a solo creator or part of a team, this extension plugs directly into your browser and elevates every AI interaction you have.

### 📚 Centralized Prompt Library

## Installation

1. Visit the [Chrome Web Store listing](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/GPT-Prompter/daojapjleapkpmoemdoapihdafcgclgd)
2. Click **"Add to Chrome"**
3. Pin the extension to your toolbar for quick access
4. Open any AI chatbot and start using your prompt library immediately

**Requirements:** Google Chrome (or any Chromium-based browser)

---

## Getting Started

After installation:

1. **Open the extension** by clicking the GPT-Prompter icon in your toolbar
2. **Browse predefined prompts** — pick from 100+ expert-crafted templates
3. **Create your own prompts** — add, edit, and categorize them in your personal library
4. **Use the Prompt Generator** — describe your goal and get a structured prompt instantly
5. **Insert into any AI tool** — works seamlessly with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more
